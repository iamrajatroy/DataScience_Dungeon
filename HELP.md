### 1. The Torch (Health)
Your life is tied to your light.

- Each room contains **3 Chests**.
- You must solve **all 3 chests** to unlock the **Portal**.

- There are **10 Rooms** total.

- **Brown Chest:** Easy (100 pts)
- **Silver Chest:** Medium (150 pts)
- **Gold Chest:** Hard (200 pts)
- **Room Multiplier:** Points are multiplied by the Room Number (e.g. Room 5 = 5x Points!).
